# Product Vision statement
**For** university students\
**Who** want a safe and convenient way to buy, sell, and exchange items and lease takeovers\
**The** ETSU Marketplace\
**Is** a university-only online marketplace\
**That** allows verified students to trade items and housing opportunities securely\
**Unlike** public marketplaces such as Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist\
**Our** product ensures trust through university-only access and a campus-focused design\
